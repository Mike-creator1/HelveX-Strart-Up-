How model selection works

When you brief a project, a senior engineer chooses the right model for your workload based on cost, latency, context length, and the regulatory profile of your data. You see the choice in the project brief before delivery starts — and you can request a different tier any time.

All models are hosted in HelveX's EU-CH-1 region by default. Private VPC and on-prem deployment are available on the Scale plan.
