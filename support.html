Common questions

How do credits work?

One credit ≈ one unit of senior engineering output. Each service has a transparent credit cost listed on its page. Credits never expire while your subscription is active.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes. You can pause for up to 90 days from the Subscriptions page. Your unused credits stay; the monthly charge stops. Active projects keep delivering.

Who actually does the work?

Senior engineers on the HelveX team. No outsourcing, no offshoring. You'll know your delivery lead by name from week one.

What happens if I'm not satisfied?

If a cycle ships and you don't accept it, we re-cycle on us. If we miss a milestone, we credit the difference back. We track this — ask for the data any time.
