Consulting.

Senior advisory engagements for founders and operators. Three formats, scoped on a call, delivered with the same hand that runs the rest of HelveX.

Engagement 01

Business Strategy

Sharpening the long-term direction of your venture: positioning, market thesis, operating model, and the few decisions that compound. Output is a written strategic frame and a quarterly milestone map you can defend to your team and your investors.

Engagement 02

Business Plan

A formal, investor-ready document covering the company, the market, the financial model, and the 18–24 month execution roadmap. Written to be defended in a room — not filed in a drawer — and delivered alongside the working spreadsheet behind every number.

Engagement 03

Sync Plan

A coordination layer for founders, internal teams, and external partners. We define the single timeline, the decision cadence, and the ownership map so delivery actually moves together — not in three parallel directions. Useful before a launch, a fundraise, or a re-org.
